Meet [Asher John Mathew] - the Photographer

Hey there! First things first, welcome to my portfolio. As the heading suggests, I'm Asher, the photographer behind all the photos you'll see or have seen below. Do check it out if you haven't yet!! I did all of my schooling in Bahrain and is right now at Ghaziabad, pursuing my Bachelors in Computer Applications. I'm also a Senior Photographer with EWL, a cell in my college and some of the photos displayed below was captured during some of the events that happened here.

My dad first gave me his Canon 60D when I was in 7th grade, and since then I've been addicted to the sound of the shutter mechanism everytime a photo is clicked. The only time a person smiles without been asked to is when a camera comes in front of them, and to be honest, when people just live in the moment, it gives the best moments and clicks. So to sum up, I just love clicking good moments and just hope that I'll have time for this passion even after college. Anyway, you guys enjoy the gallery below and I'll take your leave and probably come back to update the site whenever there's a new photo to upload. BYEEEEE!!!
