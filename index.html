Meet The Photographer

Asher John Mathew

Hey there! First things first, WELCOME! As the heading suggests, I'm Asher, the photographer behind all the photos you'll see or have seen below. Do check it out if you haven't yet!! I did all of my schooling in Bahrain and am right now in Ghaziabad, pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications. I'm also a Senior Photographer with EWL, a cell in my college, and some of the photos displayed below were captured during some of the events that happened here.

My dad first gave me his Canon 60D when I was in 7th grade, and since then I've been addicted to the sound of the shutter mechanism every time a photo is clicked. The only time a person smiles or poses without being asked is when a camera comes in front of them, and to be honest, when people just live in the moment, it gives the best moments and clicks. So to sum up, I just love clicking good moments and just hope that I'll have time for this passion even after college. Anyway, you guys enjoy the gallery below, and I'll take your leave and probably come back to update the site whenever there's a new photo to upload. BYEEEEE!!!
